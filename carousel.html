Graphic Design · Poster Series

Everyone Has A Part To Play

A five-poster series exploring community in a school grades K - 12 — each poster represents one school ending with the whole campus as one. The colour is on brand with the schools chosen palette and this piece was published and put up in the Alumni Way Hallway at Hillfield Strathalan College.
